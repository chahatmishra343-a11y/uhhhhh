### ✨ The Matrix Features

* **🖐️ Neural Gesture AI:** Powered by real-time machine learning, the engine tracks 21 distinct 3D landmarks on your hand to read your exact finger postures and pinch distances instantly.
* **✍️ Air-Draw & Cognition (Index Finger):** Point your index finger to sketch glowing neon trails. The *Intelligent Shape Cognition Engine* automatically analyzes your stroke geometry and converts scribbles into perfect physical boxes or circles.
* **🤏 Force Grab (Pinch):** Pinch your index and middle fingers together to physically grab the neon shapes out of thin air and drag them around the matrix in real-time.
* **🍎 Zero-G Toggle (Pinky Out):** Stick your pinky out to trigger the global gravity system. Shapes instantly gain mass, fall to the ground, and bounce with calculated energy decay.
* **🎛️ Glassmorphism HUD:** A sleek, blur-heavy control dashboard hovering at the top to track your current AI state (DRAW, DRAG, GRAVITY) and swap neon brush colors on the fly.

### 💻 The Tech Stack (How it was cooked)

* **Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS:** Pure code. No heavy frontend frameworks to bottleneck the rendering loop. 
* **[MediaPipe Hands AI](https://google.github.io/mediapipe/):** The absolute beast of a machine-learning model tracking your skeletal joints locally in the browser.
* **HTML5 Canvas 2D API:** The rendering engine powering the neon `shadowBlur` effects, stroke tracing, and dual-layer tracking overlays.
* **Custom Physics & Math Engine:** Ground-up collision detection (`Math.hypot`), shape bounding-box logic, and velocity/gravity acceleration algorithms.
